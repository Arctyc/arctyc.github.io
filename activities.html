Explore Thrills and Beauty Like Never Before

Experience unforgettable adventures in nature from the blue sky, the green treetops, and the clear ocean water, Taniti's world-class tour and experience guides have the perfect way for everyone to relax and take in the stunning tropical views.

Helicopter Tours

Experience Taniti from the sky with our world-class helicopter tours. Climb aboard one of our Airbus H125 sightseeing helicopters and let our tour pilots take you on an adventure you'll never forget!

Helicopter tours start at $229.99 per person. Premium and private tours are available upon request.

Kids under 10 fly free with an accompanying adult on standard tours.

Rainforest Zip-line

Exhiliration! Speed! Freedom!

Zip through the trees with a once-in-a-lifetime rush as you feel the fresh rainforest breeze blow all your worries away. Our rainforest zip-line attraction offers a safe, scenic, and unforgettable way to experience the island of Taniti.

Adults: $29.99 - Kids 8-12: $22.99.

Chartered Fishing Tours

If you're looking for some reel action, the chartered fishing tours available out of stunning Yellow Leaf Bay have everything you need. Let one of our local boat captaians guide you around the coast and share with you the stories and culture that have shaped Taniti into a true island adventure paradise.

Half day: $259.99/person - Full day: $359.99/person (All equipment included!)
